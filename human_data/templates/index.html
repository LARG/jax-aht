How to play Level-Based Foraging

Level-Based Foraging (LBF) is a cooperative game where you work together with an AI partner to collect food items on a grid.

Objective

Collect all the food items on the grid to maximize your team's score. The game ends when all food is collected or the maximum number of steps is reached.

The Level System

Every agent and food item has a level. To collect a food item, the combined levels of all agents adjacent to the food that are also attempting to collect the food must be greater than or equal to the food's level.

Example: A Level 1 player (you) and Level 1 AI partner can work together to collect Level 2 food (1 + 1 ≥ 2)

Controls

  • W / ↑ — Move Up
  • S / ↓ — Move Down
  • A / ← — Move Left
  • D / → — Move Right
  • SPACE — Collect food (when adjacent)
  • Q — Wait (skip turn)
